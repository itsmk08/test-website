About Us

What do you want to know?

Eitr Alward Technical Services LLC, located in Dubai Industrial City, UAE, is dedicated to providing reliable and efficient manpower solutions. Specializing in recruiting skilled Nepali workers, we partner with The Power House Overseas Concern Pvt. Ltd. to streamline recruitment processes, handle documentation, and ensure compliance with labor regulations in both UAE and Nepal.

We cater to industries requiring skilled professionals such as mechanics, plumbers, carpenters, and more. Our mission is to connect businesses with talent while empowering individuals to pursue global opportunities. With a strong focus on ethical practices and quality, we have built long-term relationships that reflect trust, professionalism, and excellence.

Eitr Alward Technical Services L.L.C specializes in providing skilled and semi-skilled workers for various fields, including:
  • Air Conditioning Mechanics
  • Plumbing
  • Carpentry
  • Electronics Assembly
  • Cooling Systems
Our goal is to deliver high-quality technical solutions tailored to meet client requirements.

Eitr Alward partners with The Power House Overseas Concern Pvt. Ltd. in Nepal to recruit dedicated Nepali workers. Our recruitment process includes:
  1. Identifying client requirements
  2. Screening and shortlisting candidates
  3. Ensuring proper documentation
  4. Facilitating smooth deployment
We ensure a seamless experience for both employers and workers.
